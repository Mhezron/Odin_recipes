Amish Slaw

Description

I live in a wonderful Amish community and the ladies here are wonderful cooks.
This is a lovely cole slaw recipe for those who like slaw without mayonnaise.

Ingredients

  • 1 medium head cabbage, cored and shredded
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon celery seed
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon prepared mustard
  • Â¾ cup vegetable oil

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, toss together the cabbage, onion, and 1 cup sugar. In a small
  2. saucepan, combine the vinegar, salt, celery seed, 1 teaspoon white sugar, mustard
  3. and oil. Bring to a boil, and cook for 3 minutes. Cool completely, then pour over
  4. cabbage mixture, and toss to coat. Refrigerate overnight for best flavor.
