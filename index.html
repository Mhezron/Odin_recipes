Ravioli lasagna

This easy lasagna is made with layers of frozen cheese ravioli in place of traditional lasagna noodles.
You’re going to love how ooey gooey cheesy this ravioli lasagna bake is!

Tasty BBQ Corn

While the corn is marinating, create a compound butter with some chili, cayenne and cumin.
Unwrap foil and place 1 tbsp of butter mixture on top of corn, then wrap back up. Cook on the grill for 20 minutes, turning every 5 minutes.

Amish Slaw

I live in a wonderful Amish community and the ladies here are wonderful cooks.
This is a lovely cole slaw recipe for those who like slaw without mayonnaise.
